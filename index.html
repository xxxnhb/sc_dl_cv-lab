The 4th TBSI Workshop on Learning Theory (TBSI-WOLT'22)

August 8-9, 2022

Shenzhen, China

The TBSI-WOLT'22 is hosted by Tsinghua-Berkeley Shenzhen Institute (TBSI).

The annual TBSI Workshop on Learning Theory aims to bring together the world's leading researchers in computer science, information theory, and related fields, as well as the next generation of outstanding young scholars, to explore deep unsolved problems about the machine learning and information theory. This year, TBSI WOLT’22 will feature talks by 10 distinguished researchers and poster presentations by students.

TBSI

Tsinghua-Berkeley Shenzhen Institute (TBSI) is jointly established in 2015 by Tsinghua University and University of California, Berkeley under the support of the Shenzhen Municipal Government. Its mission is to pursue interdisciplinary research, international education and industrial partnership, and it is committed to cultivating collaboration between academia, industry and government.
