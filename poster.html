Student Posters Session

OVERVIEW
In this workshop, we also want to give students a chance to present their work to experts and peers, and receive creative insights. We explicitly encourage submissions of revolutionary concepts, path breaking ideas, and ongoing research prototypes.

Due to the global COVID-19 travel restrictions, accepted posters can be presented remotely. We encourage local students from Shenzhen to present on site for the best conference experience.

IMPORTANT DATES
Submission Deadline: July 25, 2022
Poster Decision Date: July 31, 2022
Presentation Day: August 9, 2022
Each deadline is 23:59:59 AoE (Anywhere on Earth) == GMT/UTC-12:00 on the stated day, no matter where the submitter is located.

TBSI

Tsinghua-Berkeley Shenzhen Institute (TBSI) is jointly established in 2015 by Tsinghua University and University of California, Berkeley under the support of the Shenzhen Municipal Government. Its mission is to pursue interdisciplinary research, international education and industrial partnership, and it is committed to cultivating collaboration between academia, industry and government.
