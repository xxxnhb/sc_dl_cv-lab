The 2nd TBSI Workshop on Learning Theory (TBSI-WOLT'20)

July 20-22, 2020

Shenzhen, China

The annual TBSI Workshop on Learning Theory aims to bring together the world's leading researchers in computer science, information theory, and related fields, as well as the next generation of outstanding young scholars, to explore deep unsolved problems about the machine learning and information theory. TBSI-WOLT aims to provide a relaxed and enthusiastic discussion environment for cutting edge research problems, in which the multi-discipline collaborations are also encouraged.

TBSI

Tsinghua-Berkeley Shenzhen Institute (TBSI) is jointly established in 2015 by Tsinghua University and University of California, Berkeley under the support of the Shenzhen Municipal Government. Its mission is to pursue interdisciplinary research, international education and industrial partnership, and it is committed to cultivating collaboration between academia, industry and government.
